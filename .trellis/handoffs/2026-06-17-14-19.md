## Decisions Made

- **Use window-scoped `before-input-event` instead of `globalShortcut`** because `globalShortcut.register('Command+R')` registers an OS-level global hotkey that swallows the shortcut for the entire system, not just Posse's own window. The app already has a `before-input-event` handler (for `Cmd+W`) that is the correct, window-scoped mechanism.
- **Hand off rather than edit directly** because the user explicitly asked for a handoff to this repo; an exploratory edit was reverted.
